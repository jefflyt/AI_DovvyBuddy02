# Product Overview Document

## 1. What the Product Is

DovvyDive is an AI-powered dive trip planning assistant that helps scuba divers discover, plan, and organize their perfect diving adventures across the Asia Pacific region through a simple chat interface.

## 2. Who It Is For

* **Recreational Divers**: From beginners looking for safe, easy spots to advanced divers seeking thrilling wreck or cave dives.
* **Dive Travelers**: Enthusiasts planning their next vacation who want to optimize their time underwater.
* **Group Organizers**: Dive clubs and travel agents coordinating complex itineraries for groups with mixed skill levels.

## 3. The Core Problem It Solves

Planning a dive trip is notoriously complex. Divers face:

* **Fragmented Information**: Reliable data on dive sites, seasons, and operators is scattered across hundreds of websites.
* **Safety Uncertainty**: It's hard to know if a specific site matches your certification level and current skills.
* **Logistical Headaches**: Coordinating tides, weather, accommodation, and boat schedules takes hours of research.

## 4. Key Features

* **Intelligent Dive Assistant**: A conversational AI that understands diving terminology and preferences.
* **Personalized Recommendations**: Suggests dive sites based on your specific certification level (PADI/SSI), interests (e.g., "macro photography", "sharks"), and budget.
* **Safety & Skill Matching**: Automatically validates if a dive site is suitable for your experience level to ensure safety.
* **Smart Itinerary Builder**: Generates day-by-day trip plans including dive schedules, surface intervals, and logistics.
* **Weather & Seasonality Insights**: Provides real-time advice on visibility, water temperature, and the best months to visit.
* **Multilingual Support**: Fully functional in both English and Chinese.

## 5. How Users Interact With It

Users simply chat with DovvyDive as if they were talking to a knowledgeable local divemaster.

1. **Ask**: "I'm an Open Water diver looking for a 4-day trip in Malaysia with turtles."
2. **Refine**: The AI asks clarifying questions about budget or dates to narrow down options.
3. **Plan**: It presents a tailored itinerary with dive sites, operators, and travel tips.
4. **Export**: Users can save or export their plan to share with friends or book with operators.
